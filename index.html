Automage

A prototype made in 5 days for Tralalère. The goal was to teach programming to children in a fun way. In Automage the player has to program his character to carry out actions and defend himself against his environment. Made in Unity with Jurassic.

Art : Valentin Besson, Célia Idri, Guillaume Rivière
Design : Florent Juchniewicz, Benjamin Leblanc, Guillaume Faguet
Programming : Thibaut Monnet, Benjamin Leblanc

Catventure : a Meowtastic Tail

A mobile RPG made in 2 weeks and published on Google Play Store in partnership with OhBibi. I programmed the whole game in 1 week and helped on the integration. Made in Unity.

Art : Boris Kolimaga, Clément Lieven, Lili Csonka
Design : Benjamin Faucher

Diffraction

A personnal project I made in Unity for my 3rd year Jury. I made an Occlusion/Diffraction audio system in C#. I wrote a paper on this (Fr only).

Patcher

A musical VR project made in Unity with SteamVR for 4 months. I programmed most of the VR interactions and made the music (with Guillaume and Théophile !), sound design, and sound programming. This project will be released on Steam this summer.

Design : Guillaume Faguet, Antoine Lechevalier, Stéphane Debank
Programming : Benjamin Blanchard, Thibaut Monnet

Sous le Cagna : Smooth Operator Edition

A game set in World War 1 about mail censorship, choices and consequences. Inspired mostly by Reigns and Papers Please. Made in Unity in 4 days.
I made a modular system of events and letters based on Unity's Scriptable Objects, some shaders, and sound design.

Art : Maxime Conquy, Anaïs Diquet
Design : Loïc Bourdrel, Erwann Depaeuw
Programming : Thibaut Monnet, David Bourel

KeyWave

My first game, made for the GGJ17. I programmed the audio vizualisation, shaders and made the music.

Shader Stuff

A collection of different experimentals works I pursue in GLSL & CG.
